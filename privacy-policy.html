Privacy Policy

ARC Coral Facility App · Nature Seychelles · Funded by SEYCCAT

Effective date: 18 April 2026

Nature Seychelles ("we", "our", "us") operates the ARC Coral Facility mobile application (the "App"), developed in support of the Aquaculture & Restoration of Coral (ARC) project and funded by the Seychelles Conservation and Climate Adaptation Trust (SEYCCAT).

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the App you agree to the terms described below.

1. Who this App is for

The ARC Coral Facility app is an internal operations tool for staff, scientists, and authorised volunteers at the ARC Coral Restoration Facility. Access is provided by facility administrators and is not open to the general public.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Account information

When a facility administrator creates your account, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your role (e.g. admin, science officer, volunteer)
  • An encrypted password (hashed — never stored in plain text)

2.2 Operational data you enter

As you use the App to manage the facility, you create records such as:

  • Task completions and maintenance logs
  • Water quality readings and tank observations
  • Spawning event records and facility notes
  • Photos attached to logs or tasks (taken via camera or selected from your photo library)
  • Calendar events and scheduled tasks you create or complete

2.3 Device and location data

  • Location (optional): If you grant permission, the App may record GPS coordinates to tag field observations. You can revoke this at any time in your device settings.
  • Camera / Photo Library (optional): Used only when you choose to capture or attach a photo to a log. No photos are accessed without your explicit action.
  • Device identifiers: We may log anonymous identifiers (e.g. device type, OS version, app version) for diagnostics and crash reports.

2.4 What we do not collect

  • We do not collect advertising identifiers.
  • We do not use third-party advertising networks.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.

3. How we use your information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Operate the ARC Coral Restoration Facility (task scheduling, logging, reporting)
  • Authenticate you and protect facility data
  • Sync your records across devices belonging to authorised team members
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Generate internal conservation reports for Nature Seychelles and SEYCCAT

4. Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure, encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Access is restricted by row-level security policies: only authenticated ARC team members can read or modify facility data.

Offline-first: The App stores your data locally on your device so it continues to work without internet. Local data is synced to the cloud when connectivity is available.

5. Who we share data with

We share facility data only with:

  • Authorised ARC staff within Nature Seychelles who need access to perform their roles.
  • Supabase Inc., our cloud infrastructure provider, as a data processor on our behalf.
  • Legal authorities, if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Nature Seychelles or its staff.

We do not share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for marketing purposes.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Withdraw consent for optional permissions (location, camera) at any time via your device settings
  • Export your personal data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at aquaculture@natureseychelles.org.

7. Data retention

We retain facility and operational data for as long as it is needed for the ARC project's conservation and reporting purposes. If your account is deactivated, we will remove personal identifiers within 90 days, while anonymised conservation records (e.g. tank observations) may be retained for long-term scientific analysis.

8. Children's privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. International users

Nature Seychelles is based in the Republic of Seychelles. If you use the App from outside Seychelles, you understand that your data may be transferred to and processed in Seychelles and in the country where our cloud provider (Supabase) operates its servers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Significant changes will also be communicated via email or within the App.
