Frequently asked questions

Who is the ARC app for?

The ARC Coral Facility app is designed for staff, scientists, and volunteers working at the ARC Coral Restoration Facility. It supports daily facility management, task scheduling, water quality logging, and team coordination.

How do I request an account?

Accounts are provisioned by ARC facility administrators. Contact your facility manager or email arc@natureseychelles.org to request access.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. All core features — logging, task completion, photo capture — work fully offline. Your data is synced automatically as soon as you reconnect to the internet.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Use the "Forgot password" link on the login screen, or contact your facility administrator to have your password reset.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We welcome your feedback. Please email arc@natureseychelles.org with a description of the issue or suggestion, the device you're using, and screenshots if possible.

How is my data stored and protected?

All facility data is encrypted in transit and stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure. Only authorised ARC personnel can access facility data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

About the project

The ARC Coral Facility app is developed in support of the ARC (Aquaculture & Restoration of Coral) project, which aims to restore degraded coral reefs in Seychelles through sexual propagation and outplanting.
